About

As N2G, the combination of Nationwide and Generali Global Corporate & Commercial provides worldwide insurance protection, raised to the power of two. The power of an exceptionally broad portfolio of products. The power of strong partnerships with brokers and agents to identify, solve and prioritize global risks for their clients. The power of Nationwide's strong U.S.-based brand recognition and distribution, combined with Generali Global Corporate & Commercial's footprint as a global insurer across a large, 160-country network.

We deliver strong underwriting expertise uniquely synergized for domestic and foreign exposures, with seamless services and global capabilities. Meeting the needs of our international clients today, tomorrow. Together.

Bill Skapof

Chief Executive Officer

Bill Skapof will lead N2G as Chief Executive Officer. Skapof joined Generali in 2015 as Head of GC&C US, Executive Vice President and Chief Underwriting Officer. Bill is a senior P&C underwriting sales executive with 30 plus years' experience developing highly successful product, distribution and sales strategies in support of global commercial markets, having established successful multinational capabilities for the most prominent insurance players in the segment.

Warren Berey

Executive Vice President, Head of Underwriting & Operations

Warren Berey

Executive Vice President, Head of Underwriting & Operations

Warren joined Generali in 2015 to launch its U.S. and foreign markets, Generali Corporate & Commercial. He brings over 25 years of experience in P&C corporate and commercial experience. Prior to joining Generali, Warren spent several years at Zurich and AIG successfully growing their global commercial operations.

Warren earned a Bachelor’s degree in pre-law from SUNY Binghamton and an MBA in Finance from New York University.

Jennifer Winters

Foreign Casualty

Jennifer Winters

Senior Vice President, Underwriting, Head of Foreign Casualty

Jennifer joined Generali’s Foreign Casualty Underwriting team in 2017. She brings over 16 years of global casualty underwriting experience with a focus on underwriting and marketing large, complex risks. Jennifer spent several years at both AIG and XL in progressing roles.

Jennifer earned a bachelor’s degree in Mathematics and Statistics with a minor in Insurance and Risk Management from Miami University. She is actively engaged with the Insurance Industry Charitable Foundation’s Northeast Board and Associate Board. She is also active with local civic boards and organizations in Summit, NJ.

N2G partners exclusively with Nationwide and Generali Global Corporate & Commercial to underwrite global commercial accounts.

At the heart of N2G is a strong, customer-centered approach, dedicated to being lifetime partners, offering trusted protection solutions and outstanding service.

Our partners include:
• Within U.S.: Select retail and wholesale brokers with deep expertise and experience with multinational accounts
• Outside U.S.: Generali Global Corporate & Commercial agents and brokers with deep expertise and experience with multinational accounts
