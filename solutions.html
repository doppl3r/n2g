Solutions

At N2G, our mission is to provide deep expertise and unparalleled service to support our multinational accounts with global solutions. Our goal is to help brokers and agents identify, solve and prioritize global risks for their clients. Our focus is on offering flexible, modular coverage:

Worldwide Solutions

Command the coverage you need with Centurion™, N2G’s modular foreign package providing solutions for the international operations and risks of U.S.-domiciled multinational companies.

N2G Centurion Foreign Package

Protect property around the world with Tribune, N2G’s single-carrier master property solution for the global, first-party exposures of U.S.-domiciled multinational risks.

N2G Tribune Global Property

Fortify your inbound coverages from foreign produced business with Elevato, N2G’s inbound coverage wraparound product providing multinational customers with a total domestic package solution.

N2G Elevato Inbound Coverage Wraparound

Rely on N2G to complete your multinational insurance portfolio with Esperto, our domestic casualty product solution for multinational businesses. Our solution is backed by deep underwriting expertise and Nationwide, a financially strong A+ A.M. Best-rated carrier.

N2G Esperto Domestic Package

Delivering solutions for the U.S. operations and risks of U.S.-domiciled, multinational companies. Offering customizable, commercial ISO-based CGL coverage incorporating the 2013 language. Guaranteed cost, SIR and deductible structures are available.

N2G General Liability and Umbrella Solutions

Serving clients with commercial ISO-based Automobile coverage, including Liability, Auto Physical Damage and Hired and Non-owned Auto for small- to medium-sized fleets for U.S. operations.

N2G Commercial Auto Solutions

Offering solutions for U.S. companies’ risks associated with work-related injury and time away from work. Statutory limits (medical, wages, etc.) and Employers’ Liability limits up to $1 million.

N2G Workers' Compensation Solutions

Providing up to $10 million of excess capacity to ensure the necessary limits are in place for a worst-case scenario. Offering the ability to schedule multiple underlying coverages, including U.S. General Liability, U.S. Commercial Auto, U.S. Employers Liability and Foreign Casualty.

N2G General Liability and Umbrella Solutions

• Dedicated specialized claim service
